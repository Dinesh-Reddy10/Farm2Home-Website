Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information Collection

We collect personal information such as name, email, address, and phone number during account registration and checkout to provide you with the best shopping experience.

Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your orders and payments
  • Communicate with you about your orders
  • Improve our services and website
  • Send you updates about new products and offers (with your consent)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with essential service providers like payment gateways and delivery partners only to complete your orders.

Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, including encryption and secure servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your data
  • Delete your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
