Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about shopping with Farmers' Market.

How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  1. Browse our products and click "Add to Cart"
  2. Review your items in the cart
  3. Proceed to checkout
  4. Enter your shipping information
  5. Choose payment method and complete your order
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
  • UPI Payments (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm)
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (Available in select areas)
What are your delivery times?

Orders are processed within 24 hours. Delivery times vary by location:

  • Metro cities: 1-2 business days
  • Other cities: 2-3 business days
  • Rural areas: 3-5 business days

You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

You can return items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund, provided:

  • Items are in original condition
  • Original packaging is intact
  • Return request is made within 48 hours of delivery

Note: Perishable items cannot be returned unless damaged or incorrect.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in several ways:

  • Use the tracking link sent to your email
  • Login to your account and check order history
  • Contact our customer support with your order number
Can I modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify your order within 30 minutes of placing it. After that, orders enter processing and cannot be changed. Please contact customer support immediately if you need to make changes.

What should I do if I receive a damaged product?

If you receive a damaged product:

  1. Take photos of the damaged item and packaging
  2. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  3. Provide your order number and photos
  4. We'll arrange a replacement or refund
